WELCOME

AMT offers innovative and cost-efficient coating and membrane solutions for a wide variety of applications and industries. A worldwide network of research and development resources assures the latest technology for customized solutions. Clean room manufacturing capabilities including GMP assure precision and consistently highest quality.

AMT’s Silglide coating is applied to biomedical substrates as a dry lubricious coating. The coating, less then a micron in thickness, is resistant a common solvents. Silglide offers superb mechanical abrasion and flex fatigue resistance and can handle thermal stresses up to 200 C. Unlike other lubricious coating on the market, Silglide coating does not need to be wetted.
