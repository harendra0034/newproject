Bio Processing

AMT offers tailored membrane surface chemistries for a range of Bioprocessing applications. The surfaces on the membranes can contain hydroxyl groups, amino groups, silicones, fluoropolymers, propylenes or grafted gels.

Applications/Process

Strong, integrally skinned outer layer coatings allow tailoring for Bioapplications:

  • CONTROLLED PORE SIZE Ultrafiltration of Biomolecules.
  • CONTROLLED PORE SIZE EXTRACTIVE Bioreactors
  • CELL CULTURE Bioreactors
  • BIOXYGENATION for long term applications
  • Custom coated Affinity membranes

Membrane Material/Construction

AMT's composite membranes are stronger than commonly used bioprocess materials. Made from FDA grade materials with zero extractables, the membranes are solvent resistant.

Technology

Advantages of AMT's technology are enhanced chemical, thermal, and long term stability due to coated composite membrane structures.

Gas Transfer

AMT offers innovative advanced composite membranes tailored to your specific gas transfer requirements. Advantages include higher selectivities, and longer membrane lifetimes due to AMT's highly sophisticated coatings.

Applications/Process

  • BLOOD GAS such as
    • blood oxygenation and carbon dioxide removal for medical devices like artificial lungs
  • GAS TRANSFER TO LIQUIDS such as
    • Ozonation of drinking water
    • Oxygenation of drinking water, fermentors or bioreactors
  • GAS ENRICHMENT such as
    • Portable oxygen for respiratory care
    • Oxygen enrichment for enhanced fuel cell output
    • Carbonation of beverages
  • DEGASSIFY LIQUIDS such as
    • Remove oxygen from water in electronics, semiconductor processes.
    • Degas beverages

Diagnostics

AMT offers Custom Surface Coated Membranes and Beads tailored to your Diagnostic Application.

Applications/Process

AMT offers coated surfaces such as:

  • PLATINUM - coated into porous beads, discs and membranes for coordinative chemistry useful for synthesis of biomolecules
  • GOLD - coated into porous beads, discs and membranes.
  • Ideal surfaces for self assembly of biomolecules.
  • Electroconductive Coatings - for development of novel Biomolecular separations/sensors
  • Gels - such as polyacryllic acid for drug release or affinity attachment
  • Passivation coatings - for nonbinding of nonspecific biomolecules, proteins.
